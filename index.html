Apple Cineplex Cinemas

Located Everywhere In The Island

Watching a movie at Apple Cineplex Cinema is certainly a thrill for the senses as it provides a classic movie-going experience in a great setting. Attending a screening at this movie theatre definitely guarantees that you are surrounded by people with a similar passion for movies, from the staff to the audience.

The cinema hall is technically equipped with excellent film and digital projectors and a dynamic sound system. With 4 halls, Apple Cineplex Cinemas is the biggest cinema complex on the island. The show times for each hall vary and depend upon the movie being screened.
